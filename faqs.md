# FAQ

## How many photos do I get?
You receive an edited gallery. Final count depends on session length, location count, and variety.

## Do you offer unlimited photos?
The gallery includes the strongest edited images from the session. This keeps delivery clean and high quality.

## What if it rains?
We can reschedule based on weather and availability.

## What should I bring?
Cap, gown, stole, cords, and any props that matter to you.

## How do I book?
Send your preferred date, package, and a rough idea of locations through the [contact page]({{ "/contact.html" | relative_url }}).

## How far in advance should I book?
Please book at least one week before your session. Earlier is better, especially during graduation season when dates fill up quickly.

## How do I receive my photos?
You'll receive a link to your edited gallery via Dropbox or iCloud once your photos are ready.

## What's the deposit?
A deposit of 25% of your session total is required to hold your date. The remainder is due at or before the session.

## Can I bring friends or family?
Absolutely — friends and family are welcome to come along to the shoot.
